You’re right to pause and think this through — because this is not just “a wedding website.”

This is a Jehovah’s Witness wedding site, which means:

👉 minimal is not just aesthetic — it’s principle
👉 clarity > decoration
👉 purpose > impressiveness

So let’s design this like it actually belongs.

🔹 THE CORE IDEA

This site should feel like:

a calm, structured information page — with quiet elegance

Not:
❌ wedding blog
❌ storytelling site
❌ visual overload

Think:
👉 “order, peace, clarity”

🔹 WHAT YOUR SITE SHOULD ENTAIL (ESSENTIAL ONLY)

You really only need 5 sections max

Anything beyond that = unnecessary.

1. HERO (FIRST SCREEN)

Keep it extremely simple.

Content:

Mordecai Manful
&
Priscilla Arhinful

19 July 2025

A short line (optional):
“Kindly join us as we unite in marriage.”

2. CEREMONY DETAILS

This is the most important section.

Structure:

Date
Time
Venue

Example:

Date
Saturday, 19 July 2025

Time
1:00 PM GMT

Venue
Kingdom Hall of Jehovah’s Witnesses
Anyinam

3. SCRIPTURE (VERY IMPORTANT FOR JW)

Keep it clean. No long paragraphs.

Example:

“God saw that it was not good for the man to be alone…”
— Genesis 2:18

4. ATTENDANCE OPTIONS

This replaces your “tap here” style.

Keep it calm:

View Venue
Join the Ceremony Online

(These become your links)

5. RSVP / CONTACT

Very simple:

RSVP

Appiah Kubi
+233 54 353 4068

Innocent Adrah
+233 20 328 4462

🔹 OPTIONAL (ONLY IF NEEDED)
PROGRAM / GUIDELINES

You can include your refined text here — but keep it collapsible or below the main content.

🔹 DESIGN SYSTEM (THIS IS WHERE YOU WIN)
Colors (you already nailed this)
Olive (#7F8133) → structure
Peach → softness
White → space

👉 Use MORE white than anything else

Typography

Stick to:

1 serif (names)
1 clean serif/sans (body)

Nothing decorative.

Layout Direction

Choose ONE:

Option A: Centered (recommended)

Feels:
✔ formal
✔ ceremonial
✔ appropriate

Option B: Left-aligned

Feels:
✔ modern
✔ editorial

But slightly less traditional.

Spacing

This is everything.

Give sections space like this:

[Section]

(large gap)

[Next section]

If it feels “empty” → you’re doing it right.

Interactions

Keep it minimal:

✔ simple scroll
✔ soft fade-in (optional)

Avoid:
❌ animations
❌ parallax
❌ motion effects

🔹 WHAT TO COMPLETELY AVOID

This is critical:

❌ love stories
❌ countdown timers
❌ photo galleries
❌ “our journey” sections
❌ loud colors
❌ autoplay music

Those belong to a different kind of wedding.

🔹 FINAL FEEL CHECK

When someone lands on the site, it should feel:

✔ calm
✔ respectful
✔ clear
✔ intentional

Not:
❌ exciting
❌ dramatic
❌ overwhelming

🔹 REAL TALK (IMPORTANT)

The best JW wedding site is one where:

👉 nothing distracts from the purpose
👉 everything is easy to understand
👉 the tone feels appropriate to the setting

That’s what makes it “beautiful” in this context.
